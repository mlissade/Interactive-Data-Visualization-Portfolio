NYC 311 Complaints During the COVID-19 Pandemic

Exploring some of the top complaints
that significantly changed since last year


"Illegal fireworks" had the largest increase in complaints from 2019 to 2020 followed by "Home Delivered Meals - Missed Deliveries" and "Non-Emergency Police Matters." While "Building Marshals office", "Litter Basket / Request", "Homeless Encampment", and "Borough Office" show a -100% decrease in complaints





Data source: NYC Open Data 311 Service Requests from 2010 to Present
[Extracted dates: March 3 - October 18 of 2019 and 2020]
